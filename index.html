Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups sugar

Recipe

  1. Prepare sugar syrup: Boil 2 cups of sugar with 1.5 cups.
  2. Make dough: Mix 1 cup all-purpose flour.
  3. Fry balls: Fry the balls in hot ghee.
